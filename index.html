About Me

Greetings, fellow tech enthusiasts! It's Mohit Soni here, ready to rock your world with my skills in web development and data analysis. I'm currently on my way to obtaining my high school diploma through Tesla STEM High School in the bustling city of Seattle, Washington, USA.

But don't let my age fool you - I have experience. I've worked alongside a University of Washington professor who specializes in Data Science. I've also had multiple Machine Learning and Web Development-related internships to really hone my skills.

But when I'm not busy coding away, I like to let loose and have a little fun. That's where Kaggle competitions come in - it's the perfect way for me to show off my tech prowess and flex my analytical muscles. Plus, I'm always working on my own personal projects - because why not?

I know I may look like your typical tech nerd, but I promise I'm not just a coding machine. I'm also a serious cricket player! I'm a member of the U17 team at the National level under the MLCA organization.

Being part of this team has taught me so much about leadership, teamwork, and perseverance. I've had the privilege of serving as Captain and Vice-Captain for regional teams across different leagues, where I've learned to motivate and communicate effectively with my teammates, identify their strengths and weaknesses, and tailor my coaching style to help them improve their game.

Of course, playing at this level requires a lot of hard work, discipline, and commitment. But it's also incredibly fulfilling and has taught me invaluable lessons that I can apply to all areas of my life. So yeah, I may code, but I can also hit a six!
